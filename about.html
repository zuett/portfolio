03 : About me

Greetings! I'm a sophomore at NYUAD, majoring in Interactive Media with a minor in Computer Science. I'm also planning to double major in Film and New Media, merging creativity with technology.

My tech journey has been exciting. From crafting VR experiences in Unity to designing interactive games using p5.js and Arduino, I've explored various platforms. Proficient in Unity, Arduino, p5.js, C, C++, Python, and JavaScript, I'm always learning.

I'm dedicated to mastering Unity, pursuing an Associate Developer degree. Beyond tech, I'm passionate about volunteering and have devoted over 150 hours to various causes. I've completed training in cross-cultural communication, bridging gaps between diverse groups.

Additionally, I've served as a Residential Assistant, organizing events and ensuring campus safety. Blending tech, creativity, and community has been incredibly rewarding. Join me on this journey as I merge innovation, storytelling, and technology to create positive change.
